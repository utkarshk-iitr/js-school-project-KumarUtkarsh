Major BMW Milestones

1916

BMW Founded

BMW was founded in Munich, Germany and it initially manufactured aircraft engines during WWI.

1933

BMW 303 & Kidney Grille

BMW debuts in iconic kidney grille design by introduction of 303, first straight six engine.

1968

BMW 2002

The BMW's 2002 created the modern sport sedan segment. It has compact design with performance.

1975

First 3 Series

The BMW 3 Series was launched as replacement for 2002 Series. It also become BMW's most successful model.

1999

BMW X5 SUV

BMW's enters into the SUV market with the X5. This created the Sports Activity Vehicle (SAV) category.
